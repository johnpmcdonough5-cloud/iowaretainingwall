Anchored retaining wall contractors

Anchored retaining walls, also known as tieback retaining walls, are a specialized solution for properties that require high-load support or are dealing with challenging soil conditions. These walls are supported by anchors consisting of cables or strips that are driven deep into the earth behind the wall to provide stability.

With a team of professional contractors, engineers, and architects, we are able to provide high quality anchored retaining walls for residential and commercial properties.

When to use an anchored retaining wall

Anchored walls are engineered to resist high lateral pressures and are the right choice in several key situations:

  1. 1.   Poor or loose soil conditions

    Anchored walls are ideal for properties with loose or challenging soil where other wall types might fail. The cables or strips driven deep into the earth provide the additional stability that standard walls cannot offer in these conditions.

  1. 2.   High load requirements

    When a situation requires greater strength and durability than standard gravity walls can provide, anchored walls are the right solution. They are specifically designed for high-load scenarios and provide superior structural support.

  2. 3.   Limited space

    Anchored walls are an effective option when space is restricted but high structural support is still necessary. Because the support comes from anchors driven into the ground rather than a wide base, they work well in tight areas.

  3. 4.   Complex design requires professional expertise

    Because of their complex design, anchored retaining walls require professional design and construction to ensure the wall can safely handle the specific load requirements of your property. Our licensed team has the experience to do this correctly.
