Cantilever retaining wall design and installation

Cantilever retaining walls are specifically designed when there is a need to resist significant lateral soil pressure. Due to their added structural support, they are suitable for larger-scale projects, including highways and bridges.

Because cantilever walls involve more complex planning and technical construction than simpler gravity walls, it is important to hire a licensed masonry contractor to ensure safety. Our team offers end-to-end services, including initial planning, design, and final installation tailored to your property.

Key advantages of cantilever retaining walls

Cantilever walls use a base slab and a single vertical stem to resist soil pressure. Here are some of the top reasons property owners and engineers choose cantilever walls:

  1. 1.   Superior load resistance

    Cantilever walls are specifically engineered to handle significant lateral soil pressure, making them the right choice when other wall types would be insufficient for the load demands of the project.

  1. 2.   Suitable for large-scale projects

    Due to their added structural support, cantilever walls are well suited for large-scale applications such as highway embankments, bridge abutments, and major commercial developments where walls need to hold back large volumes of soil.

  2. 3.   Material versatility

    Cantilever retaining walls can be constructed from several materials including concrete, block, and brick. This versatility allows them to be adapted to both functional and aesthetic requirements of a project.

  3. 4.   Professional design and installation

    Because they involve more complex planning and technical construction than simpler gravity walls, it is essential to hire a licensed masonry contractor. Our team provides full planning, design, and installation services to ensure your wall is safe and built to last.
