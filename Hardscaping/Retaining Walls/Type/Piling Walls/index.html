Piling retaining wall design and installation

Piling retaining walls are known for providing greater stability and durability than many other wall types. They are particularly effective in properties where the soil conditions make traditional footing-based walls less practical or reliable.

Our team specializes in the design and construction of piling walls for both residential and commercial projects. We assess your site conditions and recommend the right approach to ensure a wall that lasts and performs over the long term.

Key advantages of piling retaining walls

Piling walls use vertical piles driven into the ground to provide structural support. Here are some of the top reasons property owners choose piling walls:

  1. 1.   Superior stability in challenging soil

    Piling walls are driven deep into the ground, making them especially effective in areas with soft, loose, or waterlogged soil where surface-based footings would be unreliable. The deep foundation provides exceptional long-term stability.

  1. 2.   Greater durability

    Piling walls often provide greater durability than footing-based options, particularly in difficult soil conditions. Because the piles are anchored deep in the ground, the wall is far less susceptible to movement caused by soil shifts or water pressure.

  2. 3.   Suitable for residential and commercial use

    Piling walls are a versatile solution used in both residential and commercial settings. From backyard terraces to large commercial embankments, piling walls can be scaled and designed to meet the demands of a wide range of projects.

  3. 4.   Professional assessment and installation

    Piling wall construction requires proper site assessment and professional expertise to select the correct pile type, depth, and spacing. Our experienced team handles every aspect of the project to ensure a safe and long-lasting result.
