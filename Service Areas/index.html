Retaining Wall & Hardscaping Service Areas in Eastern Iowa

Local crews building retaining walls, paver patios, walkways and driveways across 18 Eastern Iowa counties.

Eastern Iowa Retaining Wall serves homeowners and businesses across the eastern half of the state — from Cedar Rapids and Iowa City up through Dubuque, Waterloo–Cedar Falls, and the river towns along the Mississippi. Our crews handle every job with the same approach: proper excavation, compacted base, real drainage, and materials matched to Iowa's freeze–thaw cycles.

Find your county below, or call (319) 320-1833 for a free on-site estimate anywhere in our service area.
